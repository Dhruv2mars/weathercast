Weathercast answers one question clearly: when will rain reach this place?

Choose a place or use your foreground location to see whether rain is approaching, its likely arrival window, expected intensity, duration, and forecast confidence for the next 120 minutes.

Coverage is shown honestly. Precision regions can use licensed radar and dense observations; other regions use Enhanced or Standard guidance. Forecast freshness and source are visible, saved places remain on your device, and no account or background location is required.

Use Weathercast to:
• Check whether rain is expected near a selected place
• See a simple 120-minute rain timeline
• Understand forecast confidence and coverage quality
• Save places locally on your device
• Schedule on-device rain reminders
• Review how Weathercast measures rain detection, start-time error, false alerts, and confidence calibration

Weathercast does not sell personal data and does not require an account. Precise location is sent only when you choose current-location forecasting and is used to return local weather guidance.

Weathercast is not an emergency-warning or life-safety service. Follow official local alerts and instructions during dangerous weather.
