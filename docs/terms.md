# Weathercast terms outline

This repository is not a substitute for jurisdiction-specific legal advice. Before distribution, publish reviewed terms that cover:

- forecasts are probabilistic and may be delayed, unavailable, or wrong;
- Weathercast is not an emergency, aviation, maritime, medical, or public-warning service;
- users must follow official civil and meteorological authority warnings;
- data-source attribution, permitted use, and geographic availability;
- acceptable use, service changes, intellectual property, disclaimers, liability, and dispute terms;
- a working operator identity, support contact, governing law, and effective date.

The in-app disclaimer already avoids safety-critical reliance. Production terms must match the deployed providers, operator, and markets.
